Privacy

Read about our privacy policy.

Your data is yours

In order to comply with the Ley Orgánica 15/1999 de Protección de Datos Personales (from now on “LOPD”), we are NOT storing any of your private data.

We inform you, that you are entitled to ask us any question regarding this through our e-mail at hola@marsbased.com (subject “[LOPD] Personal data privacy.”)

Cookies

In this website, we are not storing any cookies that might potentially reveal any of your private data. The only cookies we're using are Google Analytics to track your behaviour in our website to help us understand what interests you and what does not.

Read more about Cookies here.

Visibility of your data

Other plugins we use on our website are Addthis and Disqus. We do not store any private data from these plugins.

Read their respective privacy policies here: Addthis privacy policy and Disqus privacy policy.

Scope

The aforementioned conditions are just valid on Earth and Mars, where we know the regulations. From here, we cannot ensure that our website is 100% compliant with Privacy regulations in other planets from this galaxy or others.
