We make the right app for you

Ruby on Rails development at its best.

MarsBased is an offshore development start-up that has two main business lines: We create our own products and we can be hired to develop mobile or web apps that meet your needs.

We mostly work with Ruby on Rails, AngularJS, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, Elastic search and other similarly awesome technologies to develop our apps. Only using the best tools can we build our killer applications!

Largely inspired by 37Signals' books "Rework", "Remote" and "Getting real", we're a lean start-up that relies on basic principles when developing: Simpler is better, working remotely and the usage of agile development methodologies to get things going.

If you want us to develop for you, send us an e-mail with your idea and we will contact you ASAP.

This is what we offer

We make everything ready to start using your app, from beginning to end.

App Design

Our killer designs are based on HTML5, CSS3 and front-end frameworks. The MarsBased designs deliver a pleasant user experience across devices and platforms using responsive design.

Development

At MarsBased we develop using Ruby on Rails & Angular JS. We boost our productivity with agile methodologies and test-driven development to offer you elegant, clean & maintainable solutions.

Deployment

Deployment and environment set-up won't be a problem for you. We prepare and automate everything for the installation & deployment of your app. Everything's stored in the cloud, accessible at all times.

Monitoring & Support

Once the app is finished and running, you can sit back and relax. We'll take care of the issues and offer customer care whenever you need it. We developed the app, so we know how it works!

Outstanding designs

Simple, elegant and smooth, the MarsBased designs deliver quality. There's more than meets the eye, though! Design and functionality go hand in hand in the MarsBased products.

Ridin' the wave

We, at MarsBased, use state-of-the-art technologies and hottest trends in our developments. In the MarsBased spaceship there's no place for obsolete technologies.

Beyond the borders

The MarsBased crew speaks many languages. Common understanding lies beyond language and physical barriers. Wherever you live, whatever language you speak, we will adapt to your needs.

Start-up Circle in Barcelona

Supporting the local scene

We have created a start-up circle with a group of local start-ups. The main purpose of the circle is to help each start-up grow through collaboration and teamwork. The circle allows each start-up to focus on their strengths, rather than to overcome their weaknesses by relying in the rest of the circle members.

If you want to read more about our start-up circle, follow our social profiles and stay tuned. Else, if you want to be a part of it, drop us a line at hola@marsbased.com.
