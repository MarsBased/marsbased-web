Mars... what?

MarsBased is a start-up created by three entrepreneurs who love building new products. Lovingly made in Barcelona.

Oops! Did we say where do we come from? We're from Barcelona - not Mars! Barely a few streets away from Sagrada Família, MarsBased HQs is where you'll find us.

MarsBased is a young offshore development start-up composed by three members. Our goal is to develop first class apps for you.

We develop software for a living: web and mobile apps, mostly based on Ruby on Rails, AngularJS and HTML5. We love it, and therefore we do it with a passion.

As an offshore development start-up, you can hire us to create apps that meet your needs from every corner of this world. Coming soon to other planets!

We haven't been around for long. In fact, it's been a few months since MarsBased was created, but we have a big expertise on app development because our previous work experiences.

But enough talking about us! We want to hear your story, because everyone has one. We deeply believe that everyone should work on what they are passionate about! Focus on what you do best, and let us develop the perfect app for you.

MarsBased style

Excellence and quality are the basis of our work. Despite being Martians we're very down to earth, and are there for you at all times. Because we work remotely, we have to build the best customer rapport.

We work locally...

Barcelona is our home, and we're involved with the local start-up community. We've created a start-up circle, we attend & organise events and support the local scene.

...while being global

We establish relationships worldwide. English is our main language but not the only one. From Barcelona to the rest of the world, we collaborate & work with people from 'round the globe.
