Mars... what?

MarsBased /mɑrzbeɪst/ (adjective)
1. Situated on Mars or has Mars as the center of operations.

Oops! Did we say where do we come from? We're from Barcelona - not Mars! The MarsBased HQs are barely a few streets away from the Sagrada Família.

We are a Ruby on Rails specialized development shop created by three entrepreneurs who love building new products. Lovingly based in Barcelona, we like to work remotely.

Our extensive background in big IT consulting firms such as Deloitte or ATOS gives us the know-how on how to get your business strategy right, perfectly aligned with our development methods.

Think of us as an external CTO, to whom you can delegate all your technical projects, tasks or questions and we'll come up with the right answer.

As a remote working company, communication is key to us. We'll invite you to our Basecamp & Trello accounts to be perfectly informed in real time of what's going on with the project.

Our team of five is distributed around the globe, but has its headquarters in the northern part of the neighborhood of Gràcia, in Barcelona.

MarsBased touch

We define quality as the ability to meet & exceed our clients' expectations, while maintaining our methodologies and inner procedures. We've always got something extra for you.

We work locally...

We're involved with the Barcelona startup community. We've created our own circle of trust with like-minded professionals, and we attend & organise events to support the local scene.

...while being global

We establish relationships worldwide. English is our main language of choice. From Barcelona to the rest of the world, we collaborate & work with people from around the globe.
